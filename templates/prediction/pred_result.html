Advice to Avoid Major Depressive Disorder (MDD)

1. Maintain a Healthy Lifestyle

  • Exercise Regularly: Engage in physical activity for at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Sleep Well: Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.

2. Stay Connected

  • Social Interaction: Spend time with friends and family.
  • Community Involvement: Participate in community activities or volunteer work.

3. Manage Stress

  • Relaxation Techniques: Practice yoga, meditation, or deep-breathing exercises.
  • Time Management: Prioritize tasks and break them into manageable steps.

4. Seek Professional Help When Needed

  • Therapy: Consider talking to a therapist or counselor.
  • Medical Advice: Consult a healthcare provider if experiencing symptoms of depression.

5. Stay Engaged in Activities

  • Hobbies: Pursue interests and hobbies that bring joy and satisfaction.
  • Learning: Keep the mind active by learning new skills or taking up new challenges.
